Privacy Policy

Futures Daily Word · Futures Church · Last updated June 2026

Futures Daily Word is a free Bible reading companion provided by Futures Church. We collect only what we need to save your reading progress, notes, and preferences across devices.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you choose to sign in, to sync your journal, plans, streak, and settings.
  • Reading activity — plans enrolled, journal entries, highlights, streak, and app preferences stored in your account.
  • Optional profile — name, campus, and profile photo if you provide them.
  • Analytics — anonymous usage events (only if you accept cookies).
  • Prayer wall posts — text you submit to your campus prayer wall, associated with your email when signed in.

How we use it

Your data is used to personalize your experience, sync across devices, send optional daily reading reminders (if you opt in), and improve the app. We do not sell your personal information.

Where data is stored

Account data is stored securely in Supabase (US). Local copies may remain on your device for offline use. Bible text and audio may be fetched from licensed third-party APIs.

Your choices

  • You can use much of the app without signing in; data stays on your device only.
  • Decline analytics cookies in the banner when prompted.
  • Disable push notifications in your device or browser settings.
  • Contact us to request deletion of your account data.
